ADMTP

Adaptive Designs and Multiple Testing Procedures Working Group.

Joint Working Group (Section) of the ROeS and the German Region of the IBS: https://www.biometrische-gesellschaft.de/arbeitsgruppen/adaptive-designs-multiple-testing-procedures.html

The annual workshop in 2024 took place in Ibiza from the 25th to 26th of April.

  • Website of the workshop https://admtp.github.io/ADMTP2024/ with photo gallery.

  • Twitter account at https://twitter.com/ADMTP_IBS

  • Press coverage at Diario de Ibiza and Nou Diari
